ABOUT DURHAM HOUSE OF PETS

HISTORY:

Durham House of Pets has been serving the animal community for 25 years! Mark Smith opened the first location in 1992 to offer only the best products and highest quality ingredients in our products. As time progessed and the company was a favorite within the community, Mark opened two other locations, one in Hampton, NH and the other in Portsmouth, NH!

WHY USE US:

Here at Durham House of Pets we promise our customers two things: superior quality and service. We know how much you value your pet and giving them the best life possible so we are proud to be the first step in providing them with high qaulity products. We also love to see the animals we're serving and have Free Treat Thursday's where you can bring in your pet for a free treat as a way to thank you for your business and to allow us to meet your beloved animal.
